About Eleven Plus

The Eleven Plus program is designed to enhance English proficiency through structured, test- based training. Our goal is to help students build essential language skills needed for academic success and competitive exams like the SAT, IELTS, CAT, and TOEFL.

What is Eleven Plus?


Eleven Plus is a comprehensive English proficiency program that combines rigorous testing with targeted learning. By improving grammar, reading, writing, and speaking, we ensure that students are well-prepared for high-stakes exams and develop a strong foundation in English.

Ideal Age Group

The ideal age range for joining the Eleven Plus program is from 4th to 8th grade. At this stage, students are at the perfect age to develop a solid understanding of English language skills, which will serve them well in both school and beyond.

How the Program Works

Classroom Training

3 hours of in-person or online lessons.

Home Self-Training

3 additional hours of self-study, including video lessons, reading exercises, and practice questions.

Mock Tests

Regular mock tests to assess and track progress, preparing students for real exam scenarios.
